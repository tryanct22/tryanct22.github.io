FOLLOW US

TRAINING

Team training sessions are held two times per week at two hours in length. Typically one session each week focuses on dribbling and footskills while the other session that week focuses on passing and receiving. Small sided games and different possession exercises are used often and sessions always end with a scrimmage.

LEAGUE PLAY

Players typically participate in 1-2 games per weekend in spring and fall. While all players participate, the amount of playing time a player receives is earned not given. FSA plays in a division against other teams at a similar level of play. We look to play competitive games on a weekly basis to promote player development.

TOURNAMENTS

Major tournaments are weekend long events which typically require Friday and Saturday night hotel stays. Additional, optional one and two day tournaments are also offered over the course of the season.
